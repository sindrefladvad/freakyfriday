Siste nytt:

Beer prices are high

KEEG festival must make money, so this year the pilsner costs 100 kroner for 0.4L, we of course use ca$hless. If you do not like beer, you can buy wine, we sell organic natural wine for NOK 200 per glass. You must top up the card with a minimum of NOK 1,000. If you do not have cover on account, we can recommend our partner mastercard. With mastercard you never run out of money and can always get drunk. The downside is that you get full of debt and worries.

The police are guarding at the festival

Whether you are a criminal, fond of drugs other than expensive beer, or generally fond of blind violence, tagging and similar hobbies, this is not the festival for you. We rather recommend you take a trip on the Danish boat, or go out to some lugubrious places in Oslo. The police are here for your safety, and line up with dogs to smell for drugs. The dogs are kind and can be petted. Police are also deploying drones to monitor and collect cool footage of moshpits.

Voulenteers at the festival

We are looking for volunteers for the festival. Application form opens soon. As a volunteer, you get a free ticket, in exchange for working 40 hours, with an average hourly wage of 40 kroner. Not tempting? Buy a ticket instead. On the other hand, we are completely dependent on volunteers to be able to make money at the festival. Cheap labor is a mainstay of the festival. Tried to work hungover before? Then this is the job for you. As a volunteer, you also get a free beer bong.
